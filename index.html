Introduction

Qur’an visualization is set to make the Qur’an easier for any user around the globe. It is also essential and instrumental to help any user who intends to memorize the whole or part of the Qur’an. It is also to help know the various events which the Qur’an illustrated.

Purpose of the project

  • a. The main purpose or objective of the project is to enlighten people to know much about the Holy Qur’an and its amazing content from a scientific perspective and more.
  • b. The relevance of this project is to attract Muslims and non-Muslims to get attached to the Qur’an. Also, to teach them the good values the Qur’an encourages and make them live a better life here and hereafter.
  • c. Here are a few tasks users can perform on our website:
    1. Navigate through images depicting related verses.
    2. Search for exegesis of some verses.
    3. Reason for the revelation of some verses.
  • d. One key functionality is the search box. This will enable users to explore verses of the Qur'an. Additionally, there are buttons like 'previous' and 'next' to help navigate back or forward to read verses related to the displayed image.
